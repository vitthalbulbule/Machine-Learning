# 🤖 AI vs ML vs DL 

Hi 👋  
This document explains the difference between Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL) in a simple way.

---

## 📌 What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the broad concept of machines being able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

### ✅ Example:
- Chatbots answering questions  
- Google Maps finding best route  
- Voice assistants like Siri  

---

## 📌 What is Machine Learning (ML)?

Machine Learning is a subset of AI where machines learn from data and improve automatically without being explicitly programmed.

### ✅ Example:
- Email spam detection  
- Netflix recommendations  
- Predicting house prices  

---

## 📌 What is Deep Learning (DL)?

Deep Learning is a subset of ML that uses neural networks (like the human brain) to solve complex problems.

### ✅ Example:
- Face recognition  
- Self-driving cars  
- Voice recognition  

---

## 📊 Difference Table

| Feature              | AI 🧠                      | ML 📊                         | DL 🧬                          |
|---------------------|---------------------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Definition          | Smart machines            | Learning from data            | Learning using neural networks |
| Scope               | Broadest                  | Subset of AI                  | Subset of ML                  |
| Data Requirement    | Low to High               | Medium to High                | Very High                     |
| Complexity          | Low to High               | Medium                        | Very High                     |
| Human Involvement   | High                      | Medium                        | Low                           |
| Examples            | Chatbots, Robotics        | Spam detection, Recommendation | Image, Speech recognition     |

---

## 🧩 Simple Analogy

- **AI** = Big circle (Goal: Make machines smart)  
- **ML** = Inside AI (Machines learn from data)  
- **DL** = Inside ML (Uses neural networks)

👉 Think like this:

AI ⭕
└── ML ⭕
└── DL ⭕
